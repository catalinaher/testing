About Consee

Consee is a dedicated platform for concertgoers to share experiences, reviews, and memories. Concert discussion is currently scattered across ticketing apps and general forums. Consee centralizes discovery + community feedback into one place.

We use external event data (ex: Songkick/Bandsintown) for concert listings and Supabase for storing user-generated reviews.
